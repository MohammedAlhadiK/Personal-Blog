By Mohammed Alhadik | Date: October 2024 | Web Development

Introduction to Web Development

Web development is a crucial aspect of modern businesses. It encompasses building websites, web applications, and maintaining their functionality.

Front-End vs Back-End Development

Understanding the difference between front-end and back-end development is key. Front-end development involves everything that users interact with, while back-end development is about databases and server-side logic.

Technologies Involved

Technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript form the core of front-end development. Back-end technologies include Node.js, Python, or Ruby. Here's an example:

                
                    <div>
                    <h1>Hello World</h1>
                    <p>Welcome to my blog</p>
                    </div>

Conclusion

Web development continues to evolve with new frameworks and technologies. Whether you're interested in front-end, back-end, or full-stack development, understanding the core concepts is crucial.
